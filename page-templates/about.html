About Paws & Purrs

My name is Lucy and I am the founder of Purrs & Paws. I have a BSc (Hons) in Animal Science. As part of my degree I studied animal behaviour, nutrition, and animal health. At the moment I have two cats, Whitney and Anthony who are rescue cats, but I have owned and cared for many other animals in the past including chickens, hamsters, rabbits, mice and fish.

I know it can be very stressful leaving your animals while you are on holiday, but with the help of Purrs & Paws, and the care plan we make together, both you and your pets can have a relaxing break.

Please see the services and prices page for more details.
