Pet Sitting Services to the South of Manchester

Many people face the problem of what to do with their much loved pets when they are going on holiday, or have to be away for work. Pets like to be at home, so they can do their own thing, no matter how odd!

Cats can find catterys stressful, and even the journey in the car to the cattery can be very stressful for them. Finding someone to look after other animals such as rabbits, chickens and guineapigs can be very difficult. Purrs & Paws provides a solution to this problem. We will come and visit your pets at home while you are away, giving them the care and attention they need.

We can look after cats, chickens, rabbits, hamsters, mice, guineapigs, fish and most other small pets. We offer a range of services that can be tailored to your needs. Please see our services and prices page for more details

Purrs & Paws will work with you to build a care plan for your pet. We can visit your pet once or twice a day. If you think your pet may need more frequent visits, this can also be discussed.

Purrs & Paws are fully insured and CRB checked. You are welcome to view these documents, please just ask. We also use unmarked vehicles, so as not to draw attention to the fact that you are on holiday.

The areas Purrs & Paws cover are: Sale, Timperley, Altrincham and Hale Barns. If your area isn't listed, please contact us, as we may be able to cover your area.
